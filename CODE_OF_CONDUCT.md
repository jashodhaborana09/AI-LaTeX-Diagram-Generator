# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Assume good intent while still addressing problems directly.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from project participation.
